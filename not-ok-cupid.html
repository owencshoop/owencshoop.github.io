Not Ok, Cupid

Not Ok Cupid is a clone of the popular dating site OK Cupid that provides users with a fun and engaging platform for finding potential mismatches. Users can create a profile, view other users, and message mismatches (people who have both disliked each other). The site also features cheeky personality questions that users can answer to provide insight into their interests and preferences. With a user-friendly interface and a wide range of features, Not Ok Cupid provides a fun and engaging experience for users looking to find and connect with others. Whether users are seeking relationships, friendship, or just a good time, Not Ok Cupid offers a unique and enjoyable platform for making connections.

Discover Page

The Discover page of Not Ok, Cupid allows users to browse and interact with other users. Users can like, dislike, or skip profiles. If two users dislike each other, a mismatch is created and they can message each other through the app. All disliked users will populate on the dislikes page. Additionally, users can update their personal information, including their age, gender, and preferred gender. The Discover page provides a fun and intuitive way for users to find potential mismatches and connect with others.

Dislikes

The Dislikes page of Not Ok, Cupid displays all of the users that the current user has disliked. This page is accessible through the dislikes tab and serves as a convenient way to keep track of users who have been rejected. In addition, a separate tab displays users who have disliked the current user. This provides users with insight into which profiles they may want to reconsider disliking in the future. The Dislikes page is an important aspect of the matchmaking process, as it helps users to focus their search and find more suitable mismatches. Overall, the Dislikes page is a useful tool for managing and organizing dislikes on the Not Ok, Cupid.

Mismatches

The Mismatches page of Not Ok, Cupid is where users can find and connect with mismatches. If two users both dislike each other, a mismatch is generated and they are able to message each other through the app. This page provides users with a unique opportunity to connect with others who don't have mutual interests, allowing them to bypass the initial step of making unlikely friends. The Mismatches page is accessible from the Mismatches tab and displays a list of the current users Mismatches. By using the mismatches page, users can quickly and easily find and communicate with mismatches, helping to facilitate deeper connections and relationships.

Sign Up

Signing up for Not Ok Cupid is simple and user-friendly. Users can create and customize their profile by selecting their gender, age, preferred genders, and age range, as well as uploading a profile picture. They have the option to update their profile at any time, and can also delete their profile if they no longer wish to use the platform. The flexible and easy-to-use profile management tools make it easy for users to get started and make the most of their experience on Not Ok Cupid.

Chat Feature

On Not Ok Cupid, users can easily message their mismatches through the app. A mismatch is created when two users both dislike each other. Once a mismatch has been established, users can access the conversation from the Mismatches page on their profile. The messaging feature allows users to communicate with their mismatches in real-time, providing them with the opportunity to get to know each other and potentially form deeper connections. The messaging feature is user-friendly and intuitive, making it easy for users to start conversations and connect with others on Not Ok Cupid.

Personality Questions

Not Ok Cupid allows users to answer personality questions, providing insight into their interests, preferences, and values. The questions are designed to be fun and cheeky, helping users to showcase their personality and connect with others who don't share similar interests. When viewing other users' profiles, the personality questions also generate a mismatch percentage, indicating the number of questions answered differently between the current user and the other user. The mismatch percentage provides users with a quick and easy way to gauge their incompatibility with other users, helping them to make informed decisions about who to connect with.
