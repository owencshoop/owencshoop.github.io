A little bit about me...

I am an early career Full-Stack Software Developer with a background as a Firefighter/Paramedic

My History

I grew up on the east coast, where I split my time between Annapolis, Maryland and Dewey Beach, Delaware. At 18, I moved to Los Angeles, California to pursue a career in emergency services. I worked as an EMT in LA County and completed various fire and emergency training programs, including El Camino Fire Academy and UCLA Daniel Freeman Paramedic School. I have an Associate of Science in Fire and Emergency Technology and hold various certificates in Urban Search and Rescue, including Rescue Systems I/II, Trench Rescue Technician, Rope Rescue Technician, and Wildland Chainsaw Faller. I served as a firefighter/paramedic in Los Angeles County. I am transitioning into a full-stack software developer after completing a rigorous 6-month full-stack software developer course at App Academy.
